Who Am I?

I'm Courtney, a graduate student in the Pensacola, Florida area. I am currently an online student through the University of Florida, pursuing a Masters of Art in Mass Communication with a specialization in Web Design and Online Communication. I graduated from the University of West Florida with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology with a specialization in Digital Enterprises. During my undergraduate years, I discovered my passion for all things digital - web design, graphic design, social media marketing, and more. I can get lost in the design and coding process, completely oblivious to the world around me and am endlessly intrigued by the power of digital platforms for business.
